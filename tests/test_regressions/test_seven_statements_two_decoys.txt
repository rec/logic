The Game of Logic

Premises:
1. All curious Bill the Lizards are sleepy Hedgehogs.
2. No vanishing Gryphons are grinning Cookmaids.
3. All late Kings of Hearts are late Hedgehogs.
4. All tea-drinking Dormice are croquet-playing Dormice.
5. All tiny Pigs are vanishing Queens of Hearts.
6. All croquet-playing Dormice are tiny Pigs.
7. No sleepy Cards are tea-drinking Dormice.
8. All late Hedgehogs are Eaglets.
9. All Eaglets are sleepy Cards.

What follows?

Answer: No late Kings of Hearts are vanishing Queens of Hearts.
